Structure of dataset¶

The dataset contains 113,937 rows and 81 columns containing the loan informations

Main feature(s) of interest¶

Loan status is the main feature of interest containing information of Loan like Chargeoff, Current, Defaulted etc.

Features in the dataset will help support investigation into Feature(s) of interest¶

Term, Loan Amount and Employment Status might be a deterministic factor in Loan Status

Conclusion¶

We obsereved that High Loan Amount are for Longer Terms and alloted to Self-Employed and Employed customers. Most of the Loan Completed by Self Employed and Employed. But we see that a high number of Defaulters having high loan amount belonging to Self-Employed Customers. Thus it's better not to allocate high Loan Amount to Self Employed Customers. Further, development like Forward Regression could be done to select the important variables to predict Loan Defaulters and further Predicting Model could be developed to find the potential Defaulters.
